Designing an Eco-Friendly Office Space

Newhall was approached by a local company looking to create an eco-friendly office space for their employees. Newhall's team of designers worked closely with the client to understand their needs and create a space that was not only environmentally friendly but also functional and aesthetically pleasing. The result was a beautifully designed office space that maximized natural light, incorporated sustainable materials, and reduced the company's carbon footprint.

Renovating a Historic Landmark

Newhall was hired to renovate a historic building in the heart of the city that had fallen into disrepair. The building had many challenges, including outdated electrical and plumbing systems, structural issues, and water damage. Newhall's team of contractors worked tirelessly to restore the building to its former glory while preserving its historical character. The result was a beautifully renovated building that stood as a testament to the city's rich history.

December 2021

Building a State-of-the-Art Healthcare Facility

Newhall was contracted to build a new state-of-the-art healthcare facility for a local hospital. The project was complex and required a high level of expertise due to the intricate plumbing and electrical systems required. Newhall's team of engineers and contractors worked closely with the hospital staff to ensure that the new facility met all of their needs while adhering to strict safety standards. The result was a modern healthcare facility that provided top-notch care to the community.
