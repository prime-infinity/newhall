About Us
We at Newhall are a team of creative thinkers who have come together to make a positive impact in the world of business and beyond.

Newhall Training and Recruitment Services is a leading provider of customized training and recruitment solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Our company was founded in 2014 with a mission to help businesses improve their hiring and training practices through customized, high-quality solutions. Since then, we have helped countless businesses find the right candidates and develop their employees' skills, resulting in increased productivity and profitability.

At Newhall Training and Recruitment Services, we offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of our clients. Our training services include customized training programs, online training modules, and leadership development workshops. We also offer recruitment services, including candidate sourcing, screening, and selection, as well as employer branding and talent management strategies.

Our team of facilitators have extensive experience in various industries and is committed to delivering high-quality services that meet the unique needs of each client. Our trainers and recruiters are skilled in identifying the skills and knowledge required for success in specific roles and industries and are dedicated to helping our clients achieve their goals.

At Newhall Training and Recruitment Services, we are committed to building long-lasting relationships with our clients. We take the time to understand their business needs and goals, and work closely with them to develop customized solutions that meet their specific requirements. Our focus on customer service and support ensures that our clients always receive the highest level of service.

We are proud to have received industry recognition for our work and have been recognized as a leader in the training and recruitment industry.

Our Services

Customized Training Programs
Our customized training programs are designed to meet the unique needs of each of our clients. We work with businesses of all sizes and industries to improve employee skills and productivity, enhance customer service, and promote overall business success. Whether it's soft skills training, technical skills training, or compliance training, we design and deliver programs that fit your specific needs and goals.
Online Training Modules
Our online training modules are a convenient and cost-effective way to provide training to your employees. We offer a wide range of modules that cover topics such as leadership development, customer service, workplace safety, and more. Our online modules are self-paced and can be accessed from anywhere, making it easy for your employees to learn and develop their skills.
Leadership Development Workshops
Our leadership development workshops are designed to help managers and executives develop the skills they need to lead their teams to success. Our workshops cover a range of topics such as communication, team building, and conflict resolution. We work with businesses to design workshops that fit their specific needs and goals.
Compliance Training
Compliance training is essential for businesses to ensure they are operating in accordance with laws and regulations. We provide customized compliance training solutions to help businesses stay compliant with laws and regulations that apply to their industry. Our compliance training programs are designed to be engaging and effective, and cover a range of topics such as data privacy, workplace safety, and anti-discrimination.
Candidate Sourcing and Selection
Finding the right candidates for your open positions can be a challenging and time-consuming process. That's where we come in. We provide customized recruitment solutions to help businesses find the right candidates for their open positions. Our recruitment services include candidate sourcing, screening, and selection, as well as employer branding and talent management strategies.
Talent Management Strategies
Retaining top talent and developing employee skills is key to the success of any business. We provide talent management strategies to help businesses do just that. Our strategies include performance management, career development planning, and succession planning. We work with businesses to develop customized strategies that fit their specific needs and goals.
